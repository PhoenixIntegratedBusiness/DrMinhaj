EXCELLENT DENTAL CLINIC &

Welcome to Excellent Dental Clinic & Orthodontic Centre, your trusted destination for advanced dental care in Ruwi, Muscat, Oman. Under the expert leadership of Dr. Akif Minhaj, who brings over 20 years of experience in dentistry, we provide a comprehensive range of services.
Our clinic specializes in dental implants (services available soon), orthodontic treatments, root canal treatments, and teeth whitening. We also offer child dental care, replacement of lost teeth, and teeth crowns (Ceramic and Zirconia). Additionally, we treat bleeding gums and pyorrhea, design stunning smiles, and offer dental jewellery for an elegant touch.
We are proud to provide professional services with Omani staff and a lady doctor available, ensuring personalized care for all. At Excellent Dental Clinic, our clinic is supported by Omani staff and a lady doctor, combining cutting-edge technology with a patient-first approach to help you achieve the healthy, radiant smile you deserve!

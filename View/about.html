About Us

Exceptional Dental Care Awaits You

Dr. Minhaj Akif Mohammed brings over 20 years of extensive experience in dentistry to our clinic. He has completed advanced courses in rotary endodontics, cosmetic dentistry, and more. Recently, he obtained his dental implant certification from the esteemed American Academy of Implant Dentistry (AAID), and we are excited to announce that he will soon be offering dental implant services at our clinic.

Dr. Akif's expertise spans all areas of dentistry, including child dental care, conservative dentistry, and the replacement of lost teeth. He is skilled in treating bleeding gums and pyorrhea, smile design, teeth whitening, and other essential dental services. His extensive experience ensures that patients of all ages receive the highest standard of care tailored to their unique needs.

In the coming months, we will introduce orthodontic treatments to correct irregularly placed teeth, further expanding our service offerings. Our clinic also features a team of Arabic-speaking staff to provide a comfortable and inclusive environment for all our patients.

We have different dental specialties, including orthodontics, with Dr. Akif serving as the manager. Additionally, we proudly have Omani staff and a lady doctor available, ensuring personalized care for every individual. We are dedicated to making your dental experience exceptional, combining cutting-edge technology with a patient-first approach to help you achieve a healthy and radiant smile.
